About Me

I am a sophomore DIGIT major at Penn State Behrend. My work explores creative coding, digital humanities, and visual storytelling. I am well versed in many applications, such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, Adobe Animate, Toon Boom Harmony, and Cinema 4D. I am also sufficient in Javascript, coding in XML, and web development. I also work in pen and ink and graphite pencil.

I am interested in building thoughtful digital experiences and combining design with technical skill. I believe deeply in art as the universal language.
